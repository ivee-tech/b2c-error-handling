Common issues
  • "We can't find that account" – check spelling or sign up.
  • "Invalid username or password" – verify caps lock.
  • Blocked account – contact support with correlation Id.
Security tips
Use a unique password and never share sign-in links. We’ll never ask for your password in email.
If you contact support, provide your correlationId (exposed in error Details when an error occurs).
